My College Days

Everything say about my experience about my college is not enough. When we entered in college campus in very fast time, we were like an discrete stones roaming into space. But after time passed on, we became like an solar system. We enjoyed together, laughed together, cried together and faced every situation together. From freshers welcome party to get-together, different festivals, fests to all the lab classes, every semester tests, grand viva, practical lab exams, going to movies after bunking classes, spending hours inside college canteen,I remember each and every day. From new relationships to heart breaks, from getting new friends to losing old ones, everything was going very smoothly. But the Corona situation came suddenly out of nowhere, we couldn't attend the college for almost like 1.5 years. That time period we lost is never comming back and we got graduated almost from home. This was a great loss of our life. During this writing, may be a drop of water came out of my eyes but no one's going to see that and no one is going to remember after five years from now for sure. That's how my college life passed by before even understanding that it is passed completely.
