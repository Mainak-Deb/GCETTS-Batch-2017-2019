My College Days

We are the unluckiest generation in terms of college life. Our college life ended up like the end of a beautiful dream. We were like a family. We were spectator of thousands of smile, crying, starting of new and hearthbreaks and what not. But I'm lucky that I have got some persons with unbreakable bonds. We realized a thing that we never give importance to a time or person until they become memories. It was our 2nd home. Thnaks to all the teachers and persons who were beside us throughout the journey.
