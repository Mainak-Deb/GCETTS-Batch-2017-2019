My College Days

It's great to know we have graduated inspite of so many odds, from network problem ...to attending lectures with video and audio switched off... to having some really "tough time" during online exams.. it was a great virtual 1.5 years. Prior to this COVID, A lot of memories has been stacked for the 2.5 year. From a very "overconfident child" from the very first day to "not so overconfident #still trying" my journey with this college ended. Yeh definately it was not without long lectures, a tons of assignments, projects, MAR and what not ... But at the end of the day this college taught me many valuable lessons , which will always be with me; made some great friends and had possibly the best 2.5 years of my life so far .. Definately I hope all my juniors reading this gets to enjoy their college in person.
