My College Days

I had enjoyed my college life to the fullest and had some best college days of my life. When I took admission in this college, I was really afraid as all the people were new to me. But soon, I started enjoying my college life and made some fantastic friends. I loved everything about my college and participated in the events at my college. Another thing I loved about my college life is Annual Fests. Every year, our college organised a massive annual fest and all the students of our college participate in various events and enjoy a lot in this fest. I have participated in my college's annual fest for all three years, and I have got the best exposure and experience of my life through this fest. I had the best time of my life in college, and my college life memories will always make me happy.
